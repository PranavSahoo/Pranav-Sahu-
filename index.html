About

He is dedicated to advancing the field of Cyber Physical System Security within Energy Infrastructure. Currently, he serves as a Project Research Fellow on the Indo-Norway extension project "Risk-Averse Resilience Framework for Critical Infrastructure Security (RARCIS)" at C3i Hub, IIT Kanpur. As a gold medalist in his M.Tech (Computer Science & Engineering), he focused on "Secure Cryptography Protocol for Vehicular Ad-Hoc Networks." His thesis introduced a lightweight Privacy Preserving Mutual Authentication and Key Distribution Protocol for VANETs, focusing on secure communication and authentication protocols.
